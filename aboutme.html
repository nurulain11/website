About Me

Hello! My name is Nurul Ain. I’m a person who cherishes meaningful connections with family and friends.

I was born on April 11, 2004, at Seberang Jaya Hospital, Penang. I am the youngest of four siblings. Growing up as the youngest, I’ve always had the support and love of my family, which has shaped me into who I am today—a person full of gratitude and dreams.

I really love cats, and I have three of them at home named Aboy, Bibi, and Miko. They each have their own personality and make my days happier. I also enjoy cooking and trying out new recipes, especially when I get to share the food with my family. I like keeping my house clean and tidy because it feels nice to have everything in order. Traveling is another thing I enjoy a lot, as it’s always exciting to visit new places, see different things, and spend time with the people I care about.

I am someone who enjoys trying new things because I believe it can provide valuable experiences and help me learn a lot in life. However, I often feel scared or hesitant whenever I want to try something outside of my comfort zone. This fear usually comes from worrying that I might not do well or make mistakes. Despite this, I try to overcome my fears by reminding myself that trying is an important part of learning and growing. Every small step I take helps me become braver and more confident in facing new challenges.
